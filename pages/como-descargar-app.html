How to download RSC Mining App

Follow the steps below. Use Next / Previous to move through the guide.

1 Open the website
Go to the RSC website in your browser — either the main homepage or the direct download page (/public/). Make sure you are on the official site.
https://yoursite.com
RSC Mining
← You should see the main page here
2 Click the download button
Find and click the “Download APK” or “Descargar App” button on the page. It is usually in the hero section or in a highlighted download area. One click will start the file download.

↑ Click this button
3 Open the file on your phone
On your Android device: open the downloaded APK from your notifications or from the Files / Downloads app. If you downloaded on a computer, transfer the APK to your phone first (e.g. USB, email, or cloud), then open it on the phone.
rsc-mining.apk
Open this file
4 Allow installation from unknown sources
If Android asks: enable “Install from unknown sources” (or “Allow from this source”) for your browser or file manager. Confirm so the installer can run. This is required for APKs outside the Play Store.
Unknown sources
Allow from this source
Enable when prompted, then tap Install.
5 You’re done
Launch RSC Mining App from your app drawer. You can now use the app and start mining from your device.
